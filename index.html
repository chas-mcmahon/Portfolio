About Me

In May 2013 I graduated from Fordham University's Honors Program, an interdisciplinary survey of Western Civilization, with a major in Classical Languages and minor in Computer Science.

Since then I have held a product management internship at 2U, where I wrote the company's style guide for web accessibliity, and have been working on expanding my knowledge of web-based programming.

Interests of mine include the ethical and societal implications of how we leverage data, ancient Athenian law, and video games, both as a potential art form and for being plain fun.
